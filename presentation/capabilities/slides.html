Background/Challenges

One of our customers received a large order for 98 road trains. While they typically assemble these road trains on-site, a shortened delivery time of just a few weeks led them to seek assistance for the 62 different tubes needed for each road train. We received the order because of a trusted relationship built on a history of promises kept. The readiness of our organization, the competency and knowledge of our sales representative, plus the skills of our assembly team enabled us to preassemble the tubing on time.

Our biggest challenge was the need to plan the hours on every single tool used in the assembling process, including setting up a schedule for cutting, deburring, bending, and preassembling sections on the shop floor. A team of seven to 11 assemblers worked more than 800 hours to bend approximately 2,100 tubes. We were able to ensure that all bends were the same and 100% repeatable by using a testing gauge.

Solution/Customer Value

The solution we provided was exactly what the customer expected. Because all they had to do was reassemble the preswaged and bended tubing, our customer could focus on the storage tanks.

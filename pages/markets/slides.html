Tube Fittings

Tube fitting performance is defined by a robust tube grip, leak-tight gas seal, and excellent vibration resistance. The proof is in our testing and actual in-plant surveying. The proved design of the Swagelok tub fitting provides excellent vibration resistance and tube support—even in harsh or high-stress nuclear environments.

Quick Connects

Expect the same quality in our line of Quick-Connects. Push-to-connect design enables quick, simple operation. No twisting, turning, or wrenching necessary—resulting in fewer leaks and greater system integrity.
